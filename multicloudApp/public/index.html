Welcome to Multicloud!

To begin, press the Sign Up button to create an account or if you already have an account, press Sign In.

Once you have signed in to our app you can manage files between multiple cloud storage services, such as DropBox, Google Drive, OneDrive and Box.

To associate services with your account, click on the button and navigate to the Manage Cloud Storage page.

Enter your cloud account details and navigate to the File Transfer page to begin managing files.
